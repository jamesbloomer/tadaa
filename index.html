About

Tadaa is a toolkit for creating audio information radiators. It consists of the core node module, a command line runner and a set of plugins.

Examples

Use Tadaa to

  • Play a sound when a bug is raised or closed in your bug tracking system
  • Play a sound when a story is finished
  • Play a sound when your favourite team scores / conceeds.
  • etc...
